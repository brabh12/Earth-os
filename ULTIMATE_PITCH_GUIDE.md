# 🌏 Earth OS: The Planetary Operating System
> **"Rebooting Earth's Environmental Defense System"**

## 1. Executive Summary (The Elevator Pitch)
**Earth OS** is a high-performance "Planetary Intelligence Platform" that bridges the gap between global satellite observation and local environmental action. By combining **Live Sentinel-2 Satellite Telemetry** with **GPT-4o AI Reasoning**, the platform automatically detects environmental threats and generates immediate, actionable intervention plans (logistics, costs, and personnel) for ground-level volunteers and NGOs.

---

## 2. The Problem (The "Inaction Gap")
The world is currently **Data-Rich but Action-Poor**.
- **The Information Silo**: NASA and the ESA have petabytes of satellite data, but it is locked behind complex GIS interfaces that local communities can't use.
- **The Reasoning Delay**: When a forest is cut or a river polluted, it takes months for NGOs to organize a response.
- **The Verification Crisis**: There is no easy way to track and prove the success of environmental missions, leading to "Greenwashing."

---

## 3. The Solution (Earth OS)
Earth OS treats the planet's health like a system monitor. It operates on a **Three-Tier Architecture**:
1.  **Observation**: Pulls multi-spectral pixels directly from the **Copernicus Sentinel-2** constellation via OGC protocols.
2.  **Reasoning**: An **AI Intelligence Core** (via OpenRouter) interprets the data to identify 5 threat types: Deforestation, Desertification, Water Scarcity, Pollution, and Climate Anomalies.
3.  **Action**: The system generates a **3-Stage Intervention Plan**, calculates **USD costs**, and produces a **PDF Mission Report** for immediate deployment.
